## ‚úÖ Implemented Features

### 1. Visual Email Preview Section (NEW)
**Location**: Between "Features" and "Privacy" sections

**What it does**:
- Shows visitors exactly what they'll receive every week
- Displays a realistic, interactive email preview in a mock email client
- Includes actual training plan format with:
  - Weekly summary (mileage, run days, recovery status)
  - Day-by-day workout breakdown
  - Pace guidance and coaching tips
  - Personalized coach's note

**Why it works**:
- **Reduces uncertainty**: Visitors see the actual product before signing up
- **Tangible value**: Makes the abstract concept of "AI training plans" concrete
- **Visual proof**: Shows professional, thoughtful design = credibility
- **Example workouts**: Demonstrates different run types (easy, tempo, long runs)

**Conversion psychology**:
- "Show, don't tell" - most powerful form of social proof
- Reduces perceived risk by revealing exactly what you get
- Creates desire through visualization

### 2. Comprehensive FAQ Section (NEW)
**Location**: After "Privacy" section, before final CTA

**8 Questions Addressed**:

1. **"Is this really free?"**
   - Kills the #1 objection
   - Explains no credit card, no trials, no hidden costs
   - Sets expectation for future premium features

2. **"Which running watches work?"**
   - Lists Garmin and Strava coverage
   - Breaks down by brand (Apple Watch, Coros, Polar, Suunto, Wahoo)
   - Reduces "will this work for me?" friction

3. **"Will you spam me?"**
   - Addresses email fatigue concern
   - One email per week guarantee
   - Easy unsubscribe promise

4. **"How does the AI actually work?"**
   - Demystifies the technology
   - Lists 7+ data points analyzed
   - Builds credibility through specificity

5. **"What if I miss a workout?"**
   - Reduces perfectionism barrier
   - Shows flexibility and understanding
   - Explains adaptive nature of plans

6. **"Can I cancel anytime?"**
   - Removes commitment fear
   - Describes instant, easy cancellation
   - Reinforces data deletion promise

7. **"I'm a beginner - will this work for me?"**
   - Addresses imposter syndrome
   - Shows plans scale to all levels
   - Mentions injury prevention for beginners

8. **"Is my data secure?"**
   - Security and privacy concerns
   - Bank-level encryption mention
   - Read-only access explanation

**Why it works**:
- **Pre-emptive objection handling**: Answers questions before they become reasons to leave
- **SEO benefits**: FAQs are rich in keywords and Google often features them
- **Trust building**: Transparency about limitations and processes
- **Conversion recovery**: Catches visitors in the "consideration" phase before bouncing

## üé® Design Principles Used

### Visual Hierarchy
- Hero CTA most prominent (largest, gradient, elevated)
- Secondary CTAs consistent (white buttons)
- Email preview stands out (shadowed, glowing border)

### Color Psychology
- **Orange/Pink/Purple gradient**: Energy, passion, creativity (running/fitness)
- **Green accents**: Trust, health, go/positive
- **White CTAs**: Clean, simple, inviting
- **Dark background**: Focus on content, premium feel

### Scannability
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Visual breaks (icons, emojis, color blocks)
- Clear section headers
- Bullet points and checkmarks

### Trust Signals
- Platform badges (Garmin, Strava logos)
- Privacy-first messaging
- Specific numbers ("7+ data points")
- Professional email design
